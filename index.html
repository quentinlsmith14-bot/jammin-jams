How it Works

The futuristic integrated speaker system fits perfectly into the SoundSuit. Users note that the speakers feel as if they weren't even there. A small battery compartment falls directly beside the speaker system with wiring going through all elements. We only use the newest, highest quality technology to provide the superior user experience to buyers. In addition to the astonishing speakers, having the hood up with the speakers provides an amazing surround sound experience.

The heating and cooling system is also integrated into the onesie, providing a comfortable temperature regardless of the weather. The system is controlled via a small panel on the battery pack, allowing users to adjust the temperature to their liking. Heating & cooling provides full body coverage from your heels to your shoulders. The Jammin' Jams team decided to use separate heating coils from cooling coils to prevent unnecessary wear and tear.
